# AI Discoverability Research 🔍

**The future of discoverability in an agent world.**

This is Ghost Team's living knowledge base on how discoverability is fundamentally changing as AI agents become the primary way people find, evaluate, and choose tools, services, and companies.

## Core Questions We're Answering

1. **How will companies get discovered?** When agents are the intermediary, traditional SEO/ads break down. What replaces them?
2. **How do agents decide what to choose?** What signals, data, and heuristics do AI agents use when selecting tools on behalf of users?
3. **What does this mean for marketing?** The entire funnel changes when your "customer" is an AI agent, not a human scrolling Google.
4. **What platforms will emerge?** What does the "Google for agents" look like? What does the "Yelp for AI tools" look like?
5. **How do you optimize for agent selection?** AEO (AI Engine Optimization) is the new SEO. What are the levers?
6. **How do you get discovered organically?** When there's no ad slot, how do you earn attention?
7. **API & service discoverability** — How do agents find and evaluate APIs? What makes one API get chosen over another?
