# Profound AEO Company Research

## Executive Summary
Profound is the market-leading Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) platform, named definitive leader in G2's Winter 2026 AEO category. They help Fortune 500 companies optimize for AI-driven search and discovery across platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, and others.

## Product Offering

### Core Platform: Answer Engine Insights
Profound's platform focuses on optimizing how AI engines select, summarize, and cite brands in conversational results. Unlike traditional SEO which optimizes for blue-link rankings, AEO focuses on what AI systems say about your brand.

### Key Features:
1. **Real-Time Monitoring**: Tracks 10+ AI engines including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, Claude, Grok, Microsoft Copilot, Meta AI, and others
2. **Citation Tracking**: Processes 5M+ daily citations across AI platforms
3. **Agent Analytics**: Connects to CDN providers (Cloudflare, Vercel, Fastly, Netlify, AWS CloudFront, Akamai) to analyze AI crawler behavior
4. **Prompt Volumes**: 130+ million real user conversations from GDPR-compliant data panels
5. **Content Optimization**: Automated gap analysis, content recommendations, and performance tracking
6. **Shopping Analytics**: Product placement insights and competitive analysis
7. **Multi-language Support**: 30+ languages with regional tracking

## Competitive Advantages (Three Proprietary Data Vectors)

1. **AI Prompts and Responses**: Millions of daily prompts to 10 major answer engines - longer operational history than any competitor
2. **Agent Analytics**: Billions of server logs processed to understand AI crawler behavior across major CDNs
3. **Real User Conversations**: 130M+ real user conversations showing actual search intent and behavior
